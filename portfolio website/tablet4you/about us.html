About us


Tablet4You is a website created by student application developers of the ROC Amstelland. We got this project from our projectleader: Aron van der Linden. We found this a chance to prove ourselves, so we decided to take on this challenge. So here we are. We think we have done a good job. The Tablet4You website is easy to use for everyone. If you get lots of questions about tablets, from customers refer them to Tablet4You, your supplier of all kinds of tablets!

Our goal


Tablet4You makes it so easy for everyone to make choices between tablets. This website is mainly meant for older people who do not have much (or no sense) of tablets. So if you have a family member (grandmother, grandfather, uncle, father, mother or even brothers and sisters) who wants to buy a tablet but do not know which tablet meets their needs, they can always come here.
